Welcome to Look@MA. This is a site for exploring Data about the towns of Massachusetts.

Currently, it's limited to plots about the schools based on publicly available data. Use the find command of your browser to search for a town name below. Click the link to find plots. To compare, just open links into new browser windows and compare side by side.

Thanks for checking it out. Please e-mail me with questions, ideas, or comments. -Tim
